We have officially launched the foundation website! It's up and running. Feel free to roam around. 
It's still in its initial stages, so don't expect a lot of stuff.

## The website is up and running!
Hey, everyone [mmcruzfoundation.org](https://www.mmcruzfoundation.org) is up and running. It's still a
rough draft, so we'll fix it once we have finalized the website assets such as the logo, fonts, colorschemes, etc. 
For now, please feel free to [look around](https://www.mmcruzfoundation.org/sitemap), but don't expect to see 
a lot of relevant content. 

## Future efforts
We will use this website to document our projects, partnerships, and donations we receive from our generous 
benefactors and promote our [cause](https://www.mmcruzfoundation.org).
We sincerely hope that you may be able to support our mission to improve the lives of disadvantaged children.

## So, now what?
During the course of the website development, there may be significant changes to the website structure, and
dead links or server mishaps may be inevitable. But please be rest assured that the main entry page, _i.e._, 
the home page, will _likely_ not change. 

We look forward to our adventures with you!
